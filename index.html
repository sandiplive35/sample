The real problem

A thumbnail can look good and still be easy to ignore.

Most weak thumbnails fail before the viewer even processes the details. The idea is unclear, the text disappears on mobile, or everything fights for attention at once.

01
⌁

Too many ideas

I reduce the thumbnail to one visual promise so the viewer understands the story instantly.

02
Aa

Weak mobile readability

I build around size, contrast and hierarchy — because most viewers see your thumbnail small.

03
◎

No curiosity gap

I create tension between the title and image without repeating the same message twice.
